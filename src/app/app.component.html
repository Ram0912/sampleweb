About

We Accomodate, Feed & Treatment children of the home WHO WE ARE? Accomdation FoodProviding Medical Centre Self-employment.

We has a volunteer religious organization and pioneer in doing service in the areas of where its functioning. It felt’s an urgency for upcoming of an orpahange for orphan, semi orphan and destitute children. It has been functioning since 2016 vide registration no. 159/2016 with Sub registrar, Registration office at Chennai. It founded by Mrs. K. Gayathri and Mr. J. Jel Salomon are the Founder cum Managing Trustee and President of this organization.

We has started with minimum of 4 inmates but later on as days go it inmates also growing and at present we have 25 inmates who are feel happy and forgetting about their past profession like working at welding shops, Mechanic shops, begging profession and etc., we are supporting the orphan Children by providing them Food, Self-employment, Clothing, Medical, and Shelter. we has been in this service through this almost 150 children benefited.
