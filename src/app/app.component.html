Enlightening lives

Orphan

These children don’t have even proper food at their homes so we provide tea with snacks in the morning, lunch at noon and again tea or coffee with snacks. Special education is also provided for children who are so low in cognitive function.

The main objective of this is to take care of the Mentally and physically differently abled children and to train them with vocational training such as - Phenoil making, computer Sambrani making, plastic basket weaving, medical cover making etc.

If you can’t feed a hundred people, then feed just one.

We have joined hands with another NGO and to organise clinic on wheels. Where a medical team visits the above said areas for providing care to those people who cannot afford basic medical needs. Medicines are given free of charge after check up by doctors.

About

Solaivanam foundation existed from the month of November 2016. It has been registered under Tamilnadu govt. The reg no is 159/2016

Where do we work - At present in Kannagi Nagar, Ezhil Nagar and Tsunami Nagar.

Later on at Semmencherry and Perumbakkam area (another slum clearance allotted houses area) The people living here are the ones who once lived in banks of Coovum river and sides of roads as encroachments.

Physiotherapy for the physically challenged and sensory integration for the mentally challenged ones affected with Autism or cerebral palsy or other neuro muscular disorders is also offered. We also teach morals and discipline for these children.

For Women - formation of SHG - Self-help groups and later opening up a savings account in a bank and making them to save money on each month then later getting them loans for starting small business like tiffin centre, saree business ,running tea shops etc.

Many have succeeded and improved their quality of life We also have an evening tuition centre and open school for drop outs of X and XII standards. We also conduct from time to time are health awareness programmes, medical camps and similar activities.
